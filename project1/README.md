### Project title:

### US Air Pollution Analysis (2022-2023)

### Project description:

This project analyzes air pollution trends across the United States using real-world Air Quality Index (AQI) data from 2022 to 2023. The dataset includes measurements of major pollutants such as Ozone (O₃), Carbon Monoxide (CO), Sulfur Dioxide (SO₂), and Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂). Our goal is to explore how air quality varies across states and over time, and identify which pollutants contribute most to overall pollution levels.

### Dataset source:

Kaggle: [U.S. Air Pollution Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/guslovesmath/us-pollution-data-200-to-2022)

### Research Questions

1. How has air quality (AQI) changed from 2022 to 2023?
2. Which states have the highest and lowest average air pollution levels?
3. Which pollutant contributes most to poor air quality?
4. How are different pollutants correlated with overall AQI?
5. What proportion of days fall into “Good,” “Moderate,” or “Unhealthy” air quality categories?
